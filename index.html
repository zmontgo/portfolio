Who are we?

The Bookery is a large community of over 800 members focused around all things written word. Created in 2020 by Zachary Montgomery and Benjamin Hollon, we are dedicated to the creation, consumption, admiration, and discussion of literature.

Striving to be the perfect home for all Bibliophiles, Bibliophages, Logophiles, and anyone who loves the written format, we provide a hub for tons of book-related content and conversations, as well as fun and off topic chats! We also host various events, such as the Read-a-Thon Weekend and Reading Wars.
